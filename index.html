How to make a Jager Mojito

ingredients

  • 3 fresh mint
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • small lime juice
  • 1.5 oz Jagermeister herbal liqueur
  • Sprite

preparation

  1. Firstly, start by muddling the mint in the simple sryup.
  2. Then add some lime juice.
  3. Add an ounce of Jagermeister, or if you want to be adventurous you could make it double!
  4. Finally, you can top it off with Sprite/7up or a soda of your choice.
  5. Enjoy responsibly!
